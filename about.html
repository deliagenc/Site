Our Story

We're On Your Side

Delivax was built by people who understand what it's really like to run a service business — the long days, the unreliable customers, the competitors who seem to always be one step ahead.

Our Mission

Level the Playing Field for Small Business Owners

Big corporations have marketing departments, massive budgets, and armies of specialists. You have a van, a team, and a phone that should be ringing more than it is.

We started Delivax to close that gap. We bring enterprise-level marketing expertise to local service businesses at a price that actually makes sense — and we measure our success by your revenue, not vanity metrics.

Why We're Different

We've Actually Run a Service Business

Our founders ran a residential cleaning company before starting Delivax. We know what it's like to be slammed in the summer and dead in the winter. We know the pain of getting ghosted on quotes. We know how it feels to watch a competitor with half your quality outrank you on Google.

That experience shapes everything we do. We don't sell you services you don't need. We tell you what's actually going to move the needle — and then we execute it.

Our Values Aren't Just Wall Art

🎯

Results First, Always

We don't get excited about impressions, reach, or click rates. We get excited when your phone rings more and your calendar fills up. Everything we do is tied to tangible outcomes for your business.

🔍

Radical Transparency

No smoke and mirrors. You see exactly where your money goes, what we're doing with it, and what results it's producing. Monthly reports in plain English — no jargon, no spin.

🤝

Long-Term Partnership

We don't churn clients — we grow with them. Our average client has been with us for 2+ years. When you win, we win. That alignment drives everything we do.

Speed Matters

We know your time is money. That's why we move fast: onboarding in 48 hours, campaigns live in a week, and we respond to messages same-day. No waiting on an account manager.
