Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does the free audit take? +
We typically deliver your audit within 24–48 hours of receiving your form. It covers your current Google rankings, website performance, competitor positioning, and top opportunities we see for your business.
What does it actually cost to work with Delivax? +
It depends on your business size and the services you need. Most of our clients start between $800–$2,500/month and scale up as they grow. We'll give you a transparent, itemized proposal after the audit — never a surprise invoice.
How quickly will I see results? +
Paid ads can start generating leads within 72 hours of launch. SEO typically takes 60–90 days to see meaningful ranking movement, with strong results by month 4–6. Most clients report a measurable increase in calls within the first 30 days.
Do I have to sign a long-term contract? +
No. We work month-to-month. We earn your business every month by delivering results. If you ever want to pause or cancel, we make it easy — no penalties, no games. We're confident you'll stay because the results speak for themselves.
Do you work with businesses outside the US? +
Our primary focus is US-based local service businesses, but we do work with select clients in Canada and Australia. If you're outside those markets, get in touch and we'll let you know if we're a good fit.
